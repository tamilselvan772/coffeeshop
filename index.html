About me

Coffee is a popular beverage prepared from roasted coffee beans. It has a distinctive, somewhat bitter flavor and is slightly acidic. The drink has a stimulating effect on humans, primarily due to its caffeine content. It has the highest sales in the world market for hot drinks. The seeds of the Coffea plant's fruits are separated to produce unroasted green coffee beans. The beans are roasted and then ground into fine particles typically steeped in hot water before being filtered out, producing a cup of coffee.
